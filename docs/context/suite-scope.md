# Government Software Suite Scope

## B&P (Budgeting & Planning)
- **Purpose**: Build + review department budgets
- **Key Features**:
  - Proposals and approvals workflow
  - Scenario planning capabilities
  - Budget revision tracking
- **Outputs**: Publications for external transparency
- **Users**: Budget Director, City Manager, Budget Analysts

## EAM (Asset Management)
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive asset lifecycle management
- **Key Features**:
  - Work orders and maintenance scheduling
  - Inspections and condition scoring
  - Asset inventory and tracking
- **Integration**: Supports GIS mapping, mobile fieldwork
- **Users**: Public Works Supervisor, Field Technicians, Parks & Recreation, Utilities

## PLC (Permitting & Licensing)
- **Purpose**: End-to-end permit and license management
- **Workflow**: Applicant intake → Review → Payment → Inspection
- **Key Features**:
  - Workflow builder for custom processes
  - Fee engine for automated calculations
  - Plan review integration
- **Users**: Public applicants, Administrative staff, Planners, Inspectors

## PRO (Procurement)
- **Purpose**: Strategic sourcing and vendor management
- **Key Features**:
  - Solicitations and bid management
  - Vendor compliance tracking
  - Contract management workflows
  - Vendor portal for external access
- **Users**: Procurement Buyers, Procurement Administrators

## FIN (Financials)
- **Purpose**: Complete financial management system
- **Core Modules**:
  - General Ledger (GL)
  - Accounts Payable (AP)
  - Accounts Receivable (AR)
  - Payroll
  - Purchasing
- **Features**: Cloud ERP designed for public sector, strong reporting and audit trails
- **Users**: Finance Director, Accounts Payable Clerks, Financial Analysts

## Cross-Suite Integration Points
- **Budget outcomes** flow from B&P to external reporting systems
- **Asset data** from EAM powers Capital Planning in B&P
- **Permit revenue** from PLC informs budget forecasting
- **Contract data** from PRO syncs to GL for payment processing
